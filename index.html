My approach

Case Study 02 · Lifecycle Marketing

A coordinated multi-channel program across email, push, and SMS

The problem

Engagement was being driven by isolated, channel-specific campaigns rather than a coordinated lifecycle strategy — messaging wasn't sequenced or tracked consistently across a base of 100,000+ users.

My approach

  • Built behavior-triggered campaigns segmented by lifecycle stage in Braze
  • Sequenced messaging across email, push, and SMS so it felt coordinated
  • Built Qlik dashboards for real-time engagement/conversion tracking
  • Ran ongoing A/B tests on subject lines, send times, channel order

Case Study 03 · Instructional Design

A hybrid onboarding program built to scale to 500+ new hires

The problem

As the CS/support team scaled, new hires ramped inconsistently — training varied between in-person and remote cohorts, with no shared curriculum or certification checkpoint.

My approach

  • Designed a structured, week-by-week hybrid curriculum
  • Paired live-facilitated sessions with async modules for consistency
  • Built role-play scenarios and knowledge checks as certification checkpoints
  • Created facilitator guides and decks for consistent multi-trainer delivery
